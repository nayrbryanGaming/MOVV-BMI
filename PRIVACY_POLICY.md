# Privacy Policy for MOVV BMI

Effective date: 2026-02-01

MOVV BMI is a Body Mass Index (BMI) calculator application designed to help users understand their BMI using World Health Organization (WHO) standards.

## Data Collection

MOVV BMI does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or sensitive user information.

The application works fully offline.

## Local Storage

The app uses local device storage (SharedPreferences) only to save:
- Last BMI result
- Previous BMI value for trend tracking

This data is stored only on the user’s device and is never sent to any server.

## No Internet Communication

MOVV BMI does not communicate with any external server or third-party service.

## No Account Required

Users are not required to create an account or provide any personal information.

## Children’s Privacy

MOVV BMI is safe for all ages. The app does not collect any data from users, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, updates will be posted on this page.
